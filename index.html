Game Rules

These are the rules of the game:

  1. Each team has 11 players.
  2. The game consists of two innings.
  3. The team with the most runs at the end of the game wins.

About the Game

The Cricket Game is a fun and exciting online game where players can challenge themselves to score big and compete for high scores. The game is easy to play and perfect for cricket fans of all ages.
